UI UX Designing

With our UI UX Designing Course, you can learn the latest practices and conventions in UI and UX by exploring various techniques in prototyping tools, web development, app, and mobile design & usability. Codebase has curated industry-relevant courses which is the perfect balance of self-paced learning and teachers' guidance. We offer a job-oriented, industry-centric curriculum laddering from learning from basic to mastering advanced levels. Come and explore the vast opportunities in UI & UX, and create a portfolio that highlights your skill to convert user research into high-fidelity interactive designs.

Benefits Of Learning UI UX Designing

The media and entertainment industries have strong demand for the fields of user interface (UI) design and user experience (UX) design. These are essential for web and app development since they have a big impact on attracting customers by offering an aesthetically pleasing, seamless, and fast experience. Through interactive examples, our UI UX Design Course will equip you with the most recent research methods, ideation techniques, and graphic design skills from scratch. You will learn how to use applications like Adobe XD, Figma, Sketch, Photoshop, and Illustrator to their full potential in our online lessons. We offer classes divided into short lectures and let you contribute to hands-on projects, and join a community of creators. .

We will teach you how to design a digital user experience that is ready for deployment. You'll start with developing familiarity and fluency with the fundamentals of design research to pinpoint the user expectations and the solutions they require. We guide you in putting your research into synthesis, and employing design sprints to generate strategies from concept to low-fidelity prototype. Last but not least, you'll discover how to upgrade your low-fidelity prototype to a high-fidelity design and optimize its performance using data. Along the process, you'll complete projects that will be included in a UX portfolio at the end of the course to highlight your skills to prospective employers.
